## Design DNA (consistent across all ages)
- **Eyes:** Bright teal-green (glow intensity increases with age)
- **Hat:** Dark forest green pointed wizard hat
- **Cloak:** Dark green/teal high collar cloak
- **Skin:** Warm olive-tan (Middle Eastern/Persian)
- **Hair:** Dark brown/black (silver appears in elder)
- **Background:** Dark teal starry sky with gold sparkle particles
- **Magic:** Gold energy (subtle in kid, powerful in elder)

## Usage Guide
- **Kid Appie:** Getting started content, tutorials, "beginner" tier
- **Adult Appie:** Main brand mascot, social media, product pages
- **Elder Appie:** Wisdom content, advanced guides, authority pieces
- **4-age grid:** Brand story, "The Wizard Who Never Sleeps" origin
